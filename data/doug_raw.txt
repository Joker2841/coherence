VEGAS PD — INCIDENT FILE #4021 — Bachelor Party Disturbance — June 27, 2025
Filing officer notes: witness statements conflict badly. Subject of interest: "Doug."

Phil (identified himself as the best man) was fairly agitated. He confirmed Doug is
the groom and that the wedding is this Sunday, so finding him is time-sensitive.
Phil says that at approximately 9 PM he saw Doug up on the hotel roof — though he
admitted he'd had "a few" by then, so take it with a grain of salt. He also went
off on a tangent about the cake order being wrong.

Stu (a dentist, kept mentioning it) flatly contradicts Phil: he swears that at
9 PM Doug was at the pool bar on the ground floor, not the roof. The next morning,
around 7 AM on the 28th, Stu called back to update us — Doug has now been found,
safe, at the wedding chapel.

Alan was mostly incoherent but recalled the bachelor party started at Caesars
Palace around 8 PM. There was also something about a tiger in the bathroom that
we're not going to get into.

Mr. Chow insisted, repeatedly, that Doug never left the hotel suite the entire
night. However, the taxi dispatch log tells a different story: a fare logged as
"Doug" was dropped at the airport at 11 PM.
